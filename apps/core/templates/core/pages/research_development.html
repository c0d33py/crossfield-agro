How we work

From lab to field to shelf.

  1. Hypothesis — formulation or agronomy idea, framed against a specific customer or crop problem.
  2. Lab development — formulation and stability work; small-scale efficacy screens.
  3. Pilot manufacture — produce a representative batch on pilot equipment; check scale-up risks.
  4. Field trial — multi-site, multi-season, blinded against current standard product.
  5. Commercial release — only after the trial data clears an internal review with operations and QA.

Partnerships
