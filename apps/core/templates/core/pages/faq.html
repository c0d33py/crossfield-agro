Common questions

Quick answers.

How do I request a quote?

Use the contact form and select Sales / Quote. We respond within one business day with pricing and availability.

What is your minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by product. Most fertilizer SKUs ship from one pallet upward; specialty inputs vary. Quote requests return the MOQ for your product.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, into selected South Asian and Gulf markets via established distributor partners. Get in touch to confirm coverage for your market.

Can I audit your manufacturing facility?

Yes. We host customer and second-party audits as part of supplier qualification. Contact us to schedule.

Where can I get a certificate of analysis?

CoAs ship with every order. Historical CoAs are retrievable against your lot code; contact your account manager or use the contact form.

Do you offer contract manufacturing?

Yes — see Services. Engagements begin with a written scope and named contacts on both sides.

How do I report a quality concern?

Email your account manager with the lot code from the bag/drum, or use the contact form selecting Technical Support. Our QA team investigates every reported issue.

Are your products registered with PARC / Pakistan regulators?

Yes, as required by product class. Registration certificates are part of the supplier qualification pack we provide on request.
