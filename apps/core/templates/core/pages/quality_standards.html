Quality framework

From inbound material to final release.

1. Inbound testing

Every incoming lot tested against specification; certificate of analysis archived against the lot ID.

2. Process control

In-process checkpoints with documented tolerances; deviations halt the batch automatically.

3. Lot release

Final-release testing on every batch before it leaves the plant; release authority rests with QA, not production.

4. Traceability

Lot codes link finished product back to raw material lot, operator, equipment, and shift.

Laboratory

In-house instrumentation, not outsourced testing.

Wet chemistry, instrumental analysis (HPLC, GC, AAS), and physical testing are all in-house. That gives us release cycles measured in hours, not days, and an internal feedback loop between QA and production.

Customer audits

You're welcome on site.

We host customer and second-party audits. Audit packs — QMS documentation, recent surveillance reports, capability data — are prepared on request as part of supplier qualification.
