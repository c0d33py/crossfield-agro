Who we are

An equipment company built around the working farm.

Crosfield Agro has been part of Pakistani agriculture since 1990 — starting with laser land leveling, growing into a full line of farm machinery, irrigation, seeds, greenhouses and solar power. We design, assemble and support equipment that has to stand up to long days, dust, water and the way farms actually operate.

We sell to owner-operators, contract leveling crews, commercial growers, cooperatives and government irrigation projects. What ties them together is the same need: kit that runs, parts when something breaks, and someone on the phone who has used the machine themselves.
