What we sell

A line built around the field.

Our catalogue covers the equipment a farm uses through the year: leveling kit before planting, tractors and implements through the season, irrigation that scales from drip lines to centre pivots, hybrid seeds matched to local conditions, greenhouses for protected cropping, and solar systems for pumping and on-farm power.

Where it makes sense, components are made locally to keep prices reasonable and spares easy to find. Where it doesn't, we distribute well-supported international brands.
