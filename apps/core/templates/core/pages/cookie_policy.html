What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember things like your session and your preferences.

What we use

  • Strictly necessary — session cookies that keep you logged in, that remember your cart, and that protect against cross-site request forgery. Cannot be disabled without breaking the site.
  • Functional — preference cookies that remember choices like language. Set only when you make those choices.
  • Analytics — aggregate page-view counting that helps us understand which pages are useful. We do not combine analytics data with personally identifying information.

Third parties

If we embed third-party content (e.g. a video, a map), that third party may set its own cookies. We choose providers that respect user privacy.

Managing cookies

Your browser lets you block or delete cookies. Blocking strictly-necessary cookies will prevent parts of this site from working — including the cart and the contact form.

Changes

We update this policy when we change how we use cookies. The date at the top reflects the latest revision.
