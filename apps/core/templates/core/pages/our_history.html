Milestones

From the first leveled field to a multi-country operation.

  1. Early laser leveling work

    Crosfield is among the first names to bring laser land leveling to Punjab fields, demonstrating sharp water savings and yield gains.

  2. 1990 — Company founded

    Formally established in Lahore to manufacture, import and service agricultural equipment for Pakistani growers.

  3. Branch network across Pakistan

    Sales and service offices opened in Multan, Sargodha, Chiniot, Jhang, D.I. Khan, Moro and Shahdadpur — putting parts and engineers close to the farm.

  4. Irrigation portfolio added

    Drip lines, sprinklers, filters and centre-pivot systems join the catalogue, designed for Pakistan's water table and field sizes.

  5. Seeds, greenhouses and solar

    Hybrid seed lines for greenhouse, open-field and major-crop production added, with greenhouse structures and solar pumping rounding out the catalogue.

  6. International expansion

    Offices opened in the United States, Uzbekistan, Nigeria and Afghanistan — supplying levelers, irrigation and tractors to growers and reconstruction programmes abroad.

  7. Online ordering launched

    Catalogue, quoting and order tracking moved online so distributors and key accounts can transact without phone tag.
