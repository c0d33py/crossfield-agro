In manufacturing

Resource intensity is a number we report.

We track water, energy, and waste per tonne of output at every plant. Targets are set annually; performance is published in our sustainability report alongside our financials.

Water

Closed-loop cooling where feasible; effluent treated to discharge standard.

Energy

Heat recovery on major process units; site-level energy KPIs reviewed monthly.

Waste

Solid waste segregation; hazardous waste handled by licensed contractors with manifest tracking.

On-farm

Inputs that improve, not deplete, the resource base.

Our agronomy team works with grower customers on soil-health programmes — nutrient planning that builds organic matter and reduces over-application. The most sustainable fertilizer is the one you don't need to apply twice.

Reporting

What we publish.

  • Annual sustainability report covering scope 1 and 2 emissions, water use, waste, and safety performance.
  • Audit reports for ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 available to customers under NDA.
  • Product-level carbon and resource intensity data available for major SKUs.
